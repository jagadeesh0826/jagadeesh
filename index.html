About Me

A short bio -


I am currently working as Assistant Professor in Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Indian Institute of Information Technology Design and Manufacturing (IIITDM) Kancheepuram, Chennai. I obtained my doctorate in Computer Science & Engineering Department of NIT Rourkela. My research interests are in Adhoc Networks,Wireless Sensor Networks and Internet of Things.
